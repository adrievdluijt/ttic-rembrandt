Rembrandt Editor

Trauma-informed content review for readers in living experience.

Rembrandt reviews writing for how usable it is by people in reduced-capacity states — grief, fear, pain, exhaustion, crisis, information overload, or the ordinary cognitive compromise of a bad day. It checks content against a UK lens (FCA Consumer Duty, ISO 22458, GDS content standards, WCAG 2.2 AA and Plain English) and equivalent EU and US frameworks, and returns a structured review with a suggested rewrite.

It is built for writers, editors and reviewers working on public-facing and regulated content.

Rembrandt is part of the trauma-informed content design practice at traumainformedcontent.com.
